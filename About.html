About Shloka

I started to teach my sister and she really enjoyed it. When I was a kid I would always watch baking shows like Martha Stewert and Ultimate Cake I started baking when I was about 8 or 9 years old. It would start of as frosting some cupcakes with my moms help. Then it would go to cakes and actually baking. My dad will bake sometimes, but when he bakes, he doesn't want my help. He doesn't like it when I help him with baking which is sad because I love baking. However I don't listen to him and I help him anyways. When I was 11, that was when I could really start baking. I would mix the batter, put it on the pan and frost the cake. Now I don't bake as much since I am really busy but when I do have time over the weekends I will bake. I'll bake cupcakes and small cakes if I have time.
